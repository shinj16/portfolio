About Me

Hello! I was born and raised from Houston, Texas & about to graduate from Stanford University. I am pursing a B.S. in Science, Technology, and Society with a focus in Innovation and Organization and a minor in Psychology. By following these pursuits, it encouraged me to explore the intersection of design, problem solving, creativity, and the future impact of innovations.

While at Stanford, I have been fortunate to be a part of multiple organizations and communities. I am a member of the Stanford Women's Tennis Team. I have taken active role in Stanford University Dance Marathon, Class Cabinet since my sophomore year, Co-President of Community Service of Stanford's Student Athlete Advisory Committee, and East Palo Alto Tennis and Tutoring. All of these experiences that I have had at Stanford has taught me how to work well in group settings, be calm under pressure, and get things done.
